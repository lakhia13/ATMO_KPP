About ATMO

ATMO is an atmospheric model designed for simulating chemical reactions and physical processes in the atmosphere. It provides tools for scientists and researchers to study atmospheric chemistry and physics through numerical simulation.

Version: 1.3 Web Interface

Features:

  • Box model and multi-level simulations
  • Gas, heterogeneous, and aqueous phase chemistry
  • Vertical mixing and surface decomposition
  • Data visualization and conversion tools
